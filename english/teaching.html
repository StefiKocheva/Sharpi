Wheel of the luck

That is an awesome way to attract your students' attention and check what they know or don't. If there are some omissions, they will be fast replaced with the needed information by an appropriate explanation. They shouldn't be scared from the wheel, either of the programming. With each spin, a new student should talk about the current topics, give good explanations, and examples. On the wheel are marked numbers from one to eight, which below you will see what they carry. The option is yours, but in case you want to use the "Wheel of luck" for games, the students can be separated into two or more teams for fun. Each right answer gains some points. In the end, the team with the most points, will be the winner one.
